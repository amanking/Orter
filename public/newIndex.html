Why to Use?

Continuous

Add ideas at any time, even weeks before you actually meet

  • Any time something happens on your team which triggers a new idea, capture it rightwaway. Take the load off your mind, no need to worry about remembering that point for the next meeting. IdeaBoardz will ensure that it is captured and presented back to everyone
  • When you allow people to add ideas at all times, they can add it close to the actual triggering events. This generally means that they would have clearer details about their points then
  • Since the ideas are already captured. Your meetings would be faster and more efficient
Distributed

Who says it is difficult to brainstorm with your colleagues who are across the ocean?

  • So many teams have compained that it is very difficult to do a retrospective or any other type of brainstorming meeting with a distributed team
  • IdeaBoardz promises to make it easier by giving both your teams a unified shared whiteboard where anyone can add their ideas and discuss them
  • No need to use video conferencing or desktop sharing software. Just plain old phone call will do
Online and Archived

Do you love to be a scribe in your meeting ? No one does. Idea Boardz will scribe without cribbing.

  • Dont waste paper, each participant can use their your own computer to jot down their ideas
  • IdeaBoardz will give you a nice PDF report of your entire meeting
  • You can go back to your previous months' meetings anytime and see what you discussed
